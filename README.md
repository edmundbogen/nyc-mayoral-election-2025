# NYC Mayoral Election 2025 - Live Tracker

A professional broadcast-style election tracker for the 2025 New York City Mayoral Election featuring real-time updates, polling data, and news coverage.

## Features

- **Live Polling Data**: Real-time candidate polling percentages with animated progress bars
- **Countdown Timer**: Days remaining until Election Day (November 4, 2025)
- **News Ticker**: Scrolling bottom ticker with latest election updates
- **Latest News Feed**: Breaking news and campaign updates
- **Key Issues**: Track the most important campaign topics
- **Broadcast-Style Design**: Professional news network aesthetic with red, white, and blue theme
- **Responsive Layout**: Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

## Election Information

- **Election Day**: November 4, 2025
- **Democratic Primary**: June 24, 2025
- **Early Voting**: Begins October 25, 2025
- **Registration Deadline**: October 10, 2025
